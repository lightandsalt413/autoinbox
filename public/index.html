How It Works

Your AI Clone in 3 Easy Steps

No technical skills needed. No downloads. Setup takes under 2 minutes.

01
Step 1

Connect Your Gmail

Enter your Gmail address and a Google App Password. This is a special 16-character code from Google — not your real password. You can revoke it anytime from your Google Account.

Your credentials are encrypted with AES-256 — the same standard used by banks.
02
Step 2

Train Your AI Clone

Paste 3-5 examples of your real email replies. The AI analyzes your writing style — your tone, greeting, sign-off, language preference, and personality — to create a unique Voice Profile.

The more samples you provide, the more accurate your AI clone becomes.
03
Step 3

Review, Approve & Send

When a new email arrives, your AI clone instantly drafts a reply that sounds exactly like you. Open your dashboard, review the draft, make edits if needed, then approve with one click.

You stay in full control. Nothing gets sent without your approval.
